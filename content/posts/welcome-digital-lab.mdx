# Welcome to SHawn Digital Lab

We are thrilled to announce the launch of **SHawn Digital Lab**, the new digital frontier for our research and development initiatives.

## Our Mission

At SHawn Lab, we operate at the intersection of two dynamic fields:
1.  **Bio-Science Research**: Unraveling the complexities of life through single-cell analysis and advanced bioinformatics.
2.  **Market Intelligence**: Applying quantitative analysis and AI to decipher market trends and investment opportunities.

## The Digital Transformation

This platform, built on **Next.js**, represents a significant leap forward in how we document, visualize, and share our work. It serves as a central hub for:
-   **Research Papers**: Accessible summaries and full texts of our latest findings.
-   **Quant Analysis Reports**: Data-driven insights into global markets.
-   **Technical Tutorials**: Sharing our knowledge in Python, R, and System Architecture.

## Tech Stack

This site is powered by a modern stack designed for performance and scalability:
-   **Framework**: Next.js 14 (App Router)
-   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
-   **Content**: MDX (Markdown + JSX)

Stay tuned for regular updates as we migrate our extensive vault of knowledge to this new digital home.
